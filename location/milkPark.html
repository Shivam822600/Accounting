Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to come into the Epping office, or can everything be done online?

Both work. Most Mill Park clients drop in once a year for a face-to-face  usually around tax time  and handle quarterly things like BAS lodgement or bookkeeping queries over email, phone, or our secure document portal. If you’d rather never visit an office, that’s completely fine too.

I've got a rental property in Mill Park — what can I actually claim?

Quite a bit, but it’s the area where most people either miss legitimate deductions or claim things that get flagged. Depreciation schedules, interest apportionment, repairs vs. improvements, and land tax all need to be handled carefully. We’ll walk through your specific situation rather than give a generic checklist.

Can you help set up a SMSF?

Yes  we handle SMSF setup, annual compliance, audit coordination and the ongoing administration. We’ll also be honest with you about whether an SMSF actually suits your situation, because for some clients the costs outweigh the benefits.

I haven't lodged a return in a few years. Can you fix that?

Yes, and this is more common than people realise. We regularly help clients catch up on multiple-year lodgements and negotiate with the ATO on payment arrangements where needed. The earlier we start, the better the outcome.
